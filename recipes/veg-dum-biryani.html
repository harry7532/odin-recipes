Veg Dum Biryani

Description

It is basically a vegetarian alternative to the popular meat-based rice biriyani dedicated to vegetarian or non-meat eaters. In other Veg Biriyani most commonly known as Veg Dum Biriyani is made with a combination of vegetables as an alternative to meat variants like chicken or mutton options. The concept of Veg Biriyani was introduced very recently but has gained a lot of popularity among both veg and non-vegetarians. It is mainly due to the combination of authentic flavor, taste, and the range of spices it has to offer.

There are myriad ways to make this simple and tasty rice-based dish, but the most common way is the Hyderabadi Dum Biriyani Recipe. In this variant, the rice and gravy are cooked separately but then layered and slow-cooked. This style of slow cooking is known as Dum cooking. This also helps to infuse a lot of flavor to the long grain rice from the gravy, yet keeping the integrity of the rice. The other popular ways include mixing rice and gravy together to make moist and flavored rice. This can be either pressure-cooked or pan-cooked.

Ingredients :

For Marination
  • 1 cup curd
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 2 chilli, chopped
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp oil
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 potato, cubed
  • 5 beans, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 3 tbsp peas
  • 10 mushroom, chopped
  • 3 tbsp mint, chopped
  • 3 tbsp coriander, chopped
For Rice
  • 2 cup basmati rice
  • water, for soaking & boiling
  • spices
  • 3 chilli, slit
  • ▢2 tbsp coriander, chopped
  • 2 tbsp mint, chopped
  • 2 tbsp salt
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp ghee
For Biryani
  • 2 tsp ghee
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 capsicum, cubed
  • fried onion
  • ½ cup saffron milk
  • 1 tsp biryani masala
  • 2 tsp ghee

Steps :

HOW TO MARINATE VEGETABLES FOR BIRYANI:
  1. Firstly, in a bowl take 1 cup curd, 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste, 2 chilli, 2 tsp lemon juice, 2 tsp oil, ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, 2 tbsp garam masala and 1 tsp salt.
  2. Whisk and mix well making sure everything is well combined.
  3. Add 2 potato, 5 beans, 1 carrot, 3 tbsp peas, 10 mushroom, 3 tbsp mint and 3 tbsp coriander.
  4. Mix well making sure everything is well combined. cover and marinate for 30 minutes.
HOW TO MAKE BIRYANI BASE:
  1. Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp ghee and 2 tbsp oil.
  2. Add 1 onion and fry until the onions turn golden brown.
  3. Add 1 capsicum and fry until the capsicum turns crunchy.
  4. Furthermore, add the prepared marination mixture and mix well.
  5. Cover and cook for 10 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked almost. keep aside.
  6. How to cook rice for biryani:
  7. Firstly, rinse and soak 2 cup basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  8. Now in a large vessel take enough water.
  9. Add mixed spices, 3 chilli, 2 tbsp coriander, 2 tbsp salt, 2 tsp lemon juice and 1 tsp ghee.
  10. Boil for 5 minutes, or until the water turns flavourful.
  11. Now add in soaked basmati rice and mix gently.
  12. Boil for 5. minutes, or until the rice is 80% cooked.
  13. Further, drain off the rice, and the rice is ready for layering in the biryani.
HOW TO LAYER THE DUM BIRYANI:
  1. Firstly, spread out the biyani gravy base onto the kadai. top with fried onion, coriander, and mint.
  2. Further top with cooked rice and level out uniformly.
  3. Also top with fried onion, coriander, and mint.
  4. Pour ½ cup saffron milk, 1 tsp biryani masala and 2 tsp ghee.
  5. Cover and seal using almunium foil.
  6. Simmer for 20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked well. make sure to rest for at least 30 minutes, before serving the veg dum biryani.
  7. Finally, enjoy veg dum biryani with salan and raita.
